Overview

Nova Workout And Gym Tracker is operated by NOVASTORE (PTY) LTD ("we", "us", "our").

You can delete your account directly in the app. If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion by email.

Option 1: Delete your account in the app

Recommended

1

Open Nova Workout And Gym Tracker

2

Go to Settings

3

Scroll to Danger Zone

4

Tap Delete Account

5

Confirm deletion

When you complete the in-app delete flow, we delete your account and associated cloud data tied to the app, subject to any limited retention required by law or for legitimate operational reasons.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, send a deletion request from the email address linked to your account.

Send your request to: Email: support@novastore.co.za
Subject: Delete my Nova Workout And Gym Tracker account

Please include:

  • The email address used to sign in
  • Your display name, if known

What data is deleted

Once your deletion request is verified and processed, we delete the data associated with your account, including:

  • Your account identifier (UID) and profile data stored for the app
  • Workout logs and training data stored in our cloud for your account
  • Streaks, goals, quests, preferences, and progression data stored for your account

What data may be retained

We may retain limited information only when required for legal, accounting, security, fraud prevention, dispute handling, or support purposes, such as:

  • Purchase or transaction records needed for tax, accounting, and dispute handling, retained for the legally required period
  • Minimal logs needed for security and abuse prevention, retained for a limited period

Payments are processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not store full card details.

Processing and retention timing

  • Account and workout data: deleted after the request is verified and processed
  • Purchase records: retained only as required by law and accounting obligations
  • Security and diagnostic logs: retained for limited periods for reliability and security

Need help?

If you have account, privacy, or deletion questions, contact support@novastore.co.za.
